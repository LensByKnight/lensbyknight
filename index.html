About Lens By Knight

Hi, I'm Lens By Knight, a young photographer focused on clean, professional product images. I love using light, reflections and composition to make everyday products look premium.

I currently work on portfolio projects and small brand shoots, building experience in e-commerce, social media content and simple studio setups.

If you’re a small business or brand looking for fresh product photos for your website or Instagram, I’d love to chat about ideas.

Based in the UK. Open to collaborations and product photography projects.
